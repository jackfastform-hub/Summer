      <h2>Adobe After Effects 1 (Motion Graphics Intro)</h2>

      <p>This project focused on learning the basics of Adobe After Effects, including keyframing, layer animation, and basic visual effects. I created a short motion graphics intro for a "Creative Design Channel" using text animations, shape layers, and background transitions.</p>

      <p>The most challenging part was syncing the animation with background music and ensuring smooth transitions between scenes. I practiced adjusting keyframe interpolation to make movements more natural, and experimented with opacity and scale animations to create depth.</p>

      <h2>After Effects Summative (Short VFX Clip)</h2>

      <p>For the summative project, I created a short VFX clip where I added animated elements to a real-life video (a walking scene in a park). I used shape layers to create floating particles, text animations that follow the camera movement, and color grading to enhance the mood.</p>

      <p>This project helped me master camera tracking, parent-child layer relationships, and effects like glow and particle systems. The final clip combines live-action footage with motion graphics to tell a simple "creative inspiration" story.</p>
