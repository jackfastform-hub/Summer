      <h2>Audio Commercial / PSA Assignment</h2>

      <p>This assignment required creating two commercials or PSAs, including writing scripts and recording my own voice-overs. This was my first time working in a professional recording studio, and the process felt quite stressful at the beginning.       <h2>Audio Editing for Film Summative (Ratatouille)</h2>

      <p>This assignment required adding appropriate sound effects to a silent film. I chose to use sound effects from Ratatouille. The most challenging part was incorporating all the details I observed, especially selecting sound effects that matched the actions on screen.</p>

      <p>For example, when seasoning was added in the film, I needed to carefully choose sounds that were similar and realistic. I also focused on using subtle background sounds to enhance the atmosphere of the scene. Although the process was complex and required careful attention, I was very satisfied with the final result.</p>
